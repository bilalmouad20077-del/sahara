Our Story

A Legacy Born
in the Sands

✦
25+ Years of Excellence

Where the Desert Whispers and Flavors Sing

Nestled among the golden dunes of the Sahara, Sahara Étoile is more than a restaurant — it is an immersive journey. Founded in 1999 by Chef Youssef Amrani, our establishment transforms the raw beauty of the desert into an unforgettable fine dining experience.

Every dish is a love letter to Morocco's rich culinary heritage, crafted with locally sourced spices, slow-cooked tagines, and flame-kissed grills. Our guests dine under a canopy of a million stars, serenaded by the silence of the Sahara and the warmth of Berber hospitality.
